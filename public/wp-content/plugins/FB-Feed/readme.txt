#---------------HOW TO USE THIS PLUGIN---------------#
1 - Activate the plugin in the plugins section in wp-admin.
2 - Enter Settings section and choose Facebook Feed Settings.
3 - Enter the accesstoken, app-secret and app-ID provided to you by https://developers.facebook.com/tools/explorer/.
3.5 - Enter the name of your facebook page for the url for example: sheepfriday will result in https:// facebook.com/sheepfriday.
4 - Create a gutenberg block at the page or post you wish to add the Facebook Feed on.
5 - Type the shortcode like this [sheep_facebookfeed feed_limit="2"] (use your own feed limit that you want).
6 - update and visit the page or post you just added and VOILA!
